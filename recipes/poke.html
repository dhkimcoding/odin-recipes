Poke

Description

Poke is a dish of diced raw fish tossed in sauce and served either as an appetizer or a main course. Poke and rice.

It's very delicious.

Ingredients

  • Ahi Tuna
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesami Oil
  • Green Onions
  • Toasted Sesami Seeds
  • Macadamia Nuts
  • Crushed Red Pepper (Optional)

Steps

  1. Gather all Ingredients.
  2. Place tuna in a medium non-reactive bowl. Add soy sauce, green onions, sesame oil, sesame seeds, macadamia nuts, and red pepper flakes; mix well. Cover and refrigerate at least 2 hours before serving.
  3. Serve over rice.
